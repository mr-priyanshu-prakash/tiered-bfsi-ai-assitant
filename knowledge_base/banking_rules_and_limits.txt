BFSI Banking Rules & Service Limits

Transaction Limits:
• ATM withdrawal limit: ₹50,000 per day (may vary by account type)
• Online transfer (IMPS): ₹2,00,000 per day
• NEFT: No minimum limit, subject to bank policies

Minimum Balance Requirements:
• Savings account: ₹10,000 (metro) / ₹5,000 (non-metro)
• Salary accounts: No minimum balance

Cheque Clearance:
• Local cheques: 1–2 working days
• Outstation cheques: 3–5 working days

Dormant Accounts:
• Accounts with no activity for 24 months are marked dormant
• Reactivation requires identity verification

Service Charges:
• Non-maintenance of minimum balance may attract penalty fees
• Replacement debit card charges may apply

Customer Support:
• 24/7 helpline available for lost card blocking and fraud reporting
