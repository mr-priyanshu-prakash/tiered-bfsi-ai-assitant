BFSI Interest Rates & EMI Guidelines

Savings Account Interest:
• Interest rates range from 2.5% to 4% per annum
• Calculated daily and credited quarterly

Fixed Deposit (FD):
• Tenure: 7 days to 10 years
• Interest rates vary between 5% and 7.5% depending on tenure
• Premature withdrawal may attract penalty

Loan Interest Rates:
• Home loans: starting from 8.25% per annum
• Personal loans: starting from 10.5% per annum
• Auto loans: starting from 9% per annum

EMI Calculation:
EMI is calculated based on:
• Principal loan amount
• Interest rate
• Loan tenure

Customers can use online EMI calculators to estimate monthly payments.

Late Payment Charges:
• EMI delays may incur penalty interest
• Continuous default may impact credit score

Prepayment Rules:
• Partial prepayment allowed after lock-in period
• Some loans may have foreclosure charges
