BFSI Nominee & Account Policy

Customers are allowed to register or update a nominee for all savings and current accounts to ensure smooth transfer of funds in case of unforeseen circumstances.

Nominee Update Rules:
• Nominee details can be updated through net banking, mobile banking, or by visiting a branch.
• Customers must provide nominee full name, date of birth, relationship, and address.
• For minors, a guardian must be assigned.
• Changes take effect within 24–48 hours after verification.

Account Closure Policy:
• Account holders must clear all dues before initiating closure.
• Remaining balance will be transferred to the registered account or issued via demand draft.
• Closure requests can be submitted online or at a branch.

KYC Compliance:
• Customers must maintain updated KYC documents.
• Non-compliance may result in temporary service restrictions.

Turnaround Times:
• Nominee update: up to 2 business days
• KYC update: up to 3 business days
• Account closure: up to 5 business days
